## What Path is

Path is the intelligence layer for stablecoin yield. Companies and asset
managers deposit idle stablecoins, and our prediction engine, Signal
Mesh, tells them where the yield is likely to go, with what confidence,
and what the rebalancing cost of moving would be.

We do not chase the highest APY. We minimise net rebalancing friction
and maximise the net yield after gas and slippage. Most existing yield
aggregators are reactive and burn returns on excessive rebalancing;
Path is predictive and confidence-scored.

Path is a protocol with an intelligence layer. It is not a fund and it
is not advisory-only.
