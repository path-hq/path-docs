---

Five steps to get oriented on Path before a partnership conversation.
Plan on about ten minutes end to end.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Read the introduction (1 min)">
    Start with [Introduction](/introduction) to understand what Path
    is, who it is for, and where it sits in the stablecoin yield stack.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read Signal Mesh (3 min)">
    [Signal Mesh](/core-concepts/signal-mesh) explains the prediction
    engine in plain language: what inputs it consumes, how confidence
    is scored, and how recommendations are explained.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read liquidity impact (2 min)">
    [Liquidity impact](/core-concepts/liquidity-impact) answers the
    deposit-at-size question: what happens to predicted APY when an
    allocator moves real volume into a pool, and where the break-even
    horizon sits.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the integration contract (5 min)">
    [Integration shape](/integrations/integration-shape) and
    [API and widget](/integrations/api-and-widget) describe the
    embed contract: how Path slots into an existing rail or surface,
    what the API returns, and what the widget renders.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reach out">
    Email
    [hello@pathprotocol.finance](mailto:hello@pathprotocol.finance)
    with your rail, surface, and approximate volume profile. We will
    route you to the right person and schedule a walkthrough scoped to
    your integration shape.
  </Step>

</Steps>
